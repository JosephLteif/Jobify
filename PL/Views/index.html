About Us


Jobify Is a cross platform job seeking app. The use of the app is to connect users (Jobseekers) with Companies (Recruiters) and make the job application and recruitment much easier for both sides.

Users can connect with other former colleagues and university partners and grow their network to get notified about new job opportunities and careers paths availability. Companies on the other hand can publish about their need for people to fill certain positions and thanks to their growing network, normal users can get suggestions about these job opportunities and can send an application as much efforts as a press of a button.
